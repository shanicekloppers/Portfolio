Webdesign Humanitas

Assignment and client

With my internship I got to do an assignment for Humanitas in collaboration with Internetdiensten Nederland. Humanitas wanted to renew their website, because the website hadn't changed since 2016. From this time on the website collected a lot of information. This resulted in the website being informative, when Humanitas wants their website to meet the needs of their users and shows their goals first. From this assignment I could make a webdesign for Humanitas.

The problem and the goal

The current website of Humanitas doesn't meet how they want to approach their audience. New users don't get encouraged enough to apply to the association. This is a missed chance to gain more participants, volunteers, members and people that donate. By making a new User Experience webdesign, the audience does get motivated to participate, volunteer, become a member or donate.

Eindresultaat

The research has shown that the website for Humanitas and their audience has to excist out of a humane and trusting experience. With humane is meant to show that the association is about people. The people of Humanitas get to be at the front. By bringing personal subjects to the surface, it is expected to bring trust to a new user.

With designing the experience goes to representation of the participants, volunteers, members and people that donate. By implementing a soundsless background video on the homepage, the user feels empathy and gets a trusting feeling for the people shown in the video. To bring the humane aspect more to the front, stories of people with their pictures involved with Humanitas gets shared over different pages.

This humane and trustful webdesign for Humanitas gets new participants, volunteerd, members and donating people more involved.
