Webdesign ContentCanvas

Assignment and client

In 2017 Kristel Witvliet and Rico den Boer founded Buzzzle. Buzzzle is a company that works on online communication for (government)organisations to help them create and plan content for specific audiences with a contentstrategy. From this point the wonderful idea came to mind to design a tool for planning content. I got to be involved to create this idea.

The problem and the Goal

The problem is that organisations currently don't have an easy tool to plan and keep up their content. This way organisations don't structure their content that helps the bussines. Buzzzle wants to create a tool where these organisations can be helped with maintaining and planning content with a contentstrategy of John Verhoeven. This way they can plan meaningful and relevant content. With analyzing the posted content, Buzzzle wants to give insights on how to improve and make the right content for a specific audience.

End result

From a beautiful collaboration with Buzzzle the online tool/website got created. By walking through a step by step contentstrategy users can plan their content. This planned content gets analysed and gives the user feedback on where to improve the given content. This way the organisation can reach their specific audiences better.
