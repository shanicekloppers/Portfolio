Photography

Fascination

During the Minor Photography in my study Communication- and Multimedia Design I was able to carry out three impressive assignments. The greatest assignment consists of a fascination. Something that fascinates you and that you can visualize. My fascination was with electric guitars. From this I was able to highlight different parts of different electric guitars. Since these electric guitars were my fathers, I am very thankful for this project.


Bodyscaping

In the second assignment I performed bodyscaping. In this, the shapes of the human body are seen by playing with light. How can the light be used in such a way that the shapes of the body are seen as just shapes.


Texture & Rythm

The third assignment speaks for itself: Texture and Rhythm. Various textures are photographed in detail here. This assignment makes the photos as sharp as possible by shooting upclose to the textures and rhythms.
