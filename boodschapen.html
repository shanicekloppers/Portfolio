App design BoodschApp

Assignment and client

While working with Online Agency I got asked to design and online application that could be used in the supermarket. The client Online Agency is specialized in building, maintaining and optimizing in websites and applications.

From this assignment, Boodschap (Groceries) App was created. This App is made for parents and their kids to have a better experience when grocery shopping. The client proposed this issue; People experience difficulties bringing their kids to the supermarket. With a fitting solution this problem could be resolved.

The problem and the goal

The problem seems to be that parents and their kids don't really enjoy going to the grocery store together. Kids experience doing groceries as being boring or none entertaining. This can result in temper tantrums or other unbearable behavior. For parents grocery shopping this way takes over too much time and have to split their attention form grocery shopping.

The goal of the client is to make an App that is an combination of Pok√©mon Go and a grocery shopping list. This application is called BoodschApp and is done for the supermarket Albert Heijn. The goal of the App is to involve kids with doing groceries. The BoodschApp makes kids motivated to help find groceries. The App also makes sure the parent can follow what their kid is doing and at the same time keep track of the grocery list. This way grocery shopping can be fun and easy for parents and their kids.
